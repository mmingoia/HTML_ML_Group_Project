Nonvoter Data: When voters don't turn out

What are the reasons why voters don't turn out?
We've got the details Here


There are several reasons why voters decide not to turn out to the polls. Based on census data, we can plot the total nonvoter turnout in different election years. We can even break down what reasons were cited in each election year.

Top 3 Reasons 2000: Too Busy, Illness or Disability, Not Interested

Top 3 Reasons 2004: Too Busy, Illness or Disability, Other Reason

Top 3 Reasons 2008: Too Busy, Illness or Disability, Not Interested

Top 3 Reasons 2012: Too Busy, Not Interested, Did Not Like Candidates

Top 3 Reasons 2016: Did Not Like Candidates, Not Interested, Too Busy
